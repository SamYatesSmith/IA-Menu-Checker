What do we do?

Our goal is to aid our users in avoiding adverse effects from people unwittingly consuming foods that deteriorate their health. Our further goal is to ensure restaurants offer maximum transparency for their menu's and allow users to be fully aware as to what they can or cannot eat.

How does it work?

We achieve the statement made in the "What We Do" section by allowing the customer to interrogate the selling company's menu. We input the menu into our clever software, and the customer then inputs their allergies and intolerances. The customer submits their preferences, and the software returns items on the menu that should be deemed safe based on the user's input.

EDIBLE Checker Information

Edible's software is not a new idea. However, there aren't programs that achieve what we do. The input of preference by the customer in order to directly assess safe consumption on a menu is a fresh approach. Within this software lies the sandwich and baugette menu for Pret a'Manger. We decided to use this menu as an example of how the software can work. It is an older menu and as of late 2023, there are new and differing options availbale to customers. Edible's 'Checker' essentially can take a menu and interrogate it for the user, allowing the user to highlight elements of the menu which are unsuitable for consumption. Pretty simple really!
