What do we do?

Our goal is to eliminate adverse effects from people unwittingly consuming foods that deteriorate their health.

How does it work?

We achieve the statement made in the "What We Do" section by allowing the customer to interrogate the selling company's menu. We input the menu into our clever software, and the customer then inputs their allergies and intolerances. The customer submits their preferences, and the software returns items on the menu that should be deemed safe based on the user's input.

I & A Information

I & A (Intolerance and Allergens) is not a new idea. However, there does not seem to be a program that achieves what we do. The input of preference by the customer in order to directly assess safe consumption on a menu is a fresh approach.
